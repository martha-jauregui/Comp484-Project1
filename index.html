What is Folklorico?

Folklorico is a vibrant traditional dance style originating from Mexico that fuses indigenous, Spanish, and local customs to represent diverse regional cultures. These performances are famously accompanied by live Mariachi music, creating a powerful synergy between the rhythmic footwork and the soaring trumpets and violins. With over 30 distinct regional styles, the dance serves as a living history book of the unique heritage found across different Mexican states. The artistic choreography is designed to convey cultural stories through highly expressive movements and theatrical flair. To enhance the sound, the dancers wear specialized shoes with nails in the heels and toes that allow them to act as percussion instruments alongside the music.

Interested in Dancing?

Think you’ve missed the boat on dancing? Think again you are never too old to step onto the floor and start learning Folklorico. It’s a fantastic way to keep your heart pumping and stay in great shape while moving to the music. The sheer joy and pride you feel while dancing is something I think everyone should experience at least once in their lives. Foklorico is growing constantly, there are many dance groups who are trying to keep the tradtion alive.
