Interested in Dancing?

Think you’ve missed the boat on dancing? Think again you are never too old to step onto the floor and start learning Folklorico. It’s a fantastic way to keep your heart pumping and stay in great shape while moving to the music. The sheer joy and pride you feel while dancing is something I think everyone should experience at least once in their lives. Foklorico is growing constantly, there are many dance groups keeping the tradtion alive.
